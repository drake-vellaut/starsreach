Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 07/02/25

1. Who We Are

This website ("Site") is operated by GalacticGeographic.org. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how we collect and use information.

2. What Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or account details.

However, we use a tracking pixel hosted on our parent website (https://gunc.org) to collect basic, non-personal data for the purpose of understanding website traffic. This includes:

  • IP address (used for approximate geolocation)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Date and time of visit
  • Page visited

This data is collected anonymously and is not linked to any individual user.

3. How We Use This Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Analyzing website traffic and performance
  • Understanding visitor behavior
  • Improving the content and usability of our site

We use AWStats and Webalizer, which are privacy-friendly, server-based analytics tools. These tools do not use cookies or JavaScript.

4. No Use of Cookies or Third-Party Trackers

  • We do not use cookies for tracking.
  • We do not use third-party analytics providers.
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties.

5. Your Privacy Choices

Since we do not use cookies or collect personal data, there is no need for cookie consent banners or opt-out mechanisms.

6. Data Retention

Server logs and analytics data are retained for a limited period (typically 30–90 days) and are used only for internal analysis.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
