## Emotes

> Full list of emotes and moods below, all you need to do for an emote is type /<emote name> and for Moods it is /mood <mood name> and to return to normal mood type /mood normal.
- /dance - when activated in a homestead or camp it heals the wounds you have received through adventuring (that grey lined bar on your bars can be replenished by dancing - only in homesteads or camps though)
