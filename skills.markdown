> Once you have selected your mineral that you wish to locate, you must switch your mode back to Extraction Mode. Once you are in extraction mode you can use E to scan for the mineral you selected and at the top of the screen you will receive feedback if you successfully found the mineral or not.

### Fabricator Tool

> Equipping the Fabricator will allow the building window to appear.

#### **Q and E Special**

> Allows you to rotate or move the object on the chosen axis

#### Useful Keys

> - Undo : Ctrl + z
- Redo : Ctrl + y
- Dupe : Ctrl + d
- Delete : DELETE Key

### Instaformer

> The instaformer allows you to convert resources in your inventory into blocks that can be placed into the world. 

> **TIP:** You have to use the Lathe crafting station to create blocks from materials. If you don't do this, you will not have any inventory to select from. This is a common issue players run into.

##### Block/Wall/Floor Modes

> Depending on the mode you selected, when you click the LMB button you can drag the mouse in Wall/Floor mode to expand the area in which you will place/delete blocks (not that even if you own multiple homesteads and they are perfectly placed, this will not go across multiple homesteads only a single homestead). In block mode it is just placing/deleting one block at a time per LMB click.

###### **Q Special** - Resource Selection

> Allows you to iterate through the resources available to you in your inventory.

###### **E Special**

> Toggle between place and destroy modes.

### Paver Tool

> Use the LMB to place your selected resource into the world and start paving. Paving is important as you as a player move faster on paved surfaces.

#### Resource Mode

##### **Q and E Special** 

> Use Q and E to iterate through all your resources until you land on the resource you wish to use for paving. Q goes through the list to the left and E goes through the list to the right.

#### Brush Size Mode

##### **Q and E Special** 

> Use Q and E to alter the dimension of the brush by using Q for one axis and E for the other axis.

## Combat

### Omniblaster Weapon

#### **E Special** - Tri-Burst

> Fire three shots that home in the same way as the single shot

#### **Q Special** - Doom Blossom

> Fires a single shot that explodes at range and creates a ring of homing projectiles that streak outward

### Drones Weapon

#### **E Special** - Orb Ring

> A circle of fireballs that appear around you. They damage any opponents that get near you, and they also block incoming projectiles

#### **Q Special** - Boomerang Burst

> Creates a cone of fireballs that streak upward and away from you...and then return back, damaging opponents on their way out AND on their way back.

### Gravity Gun Weapon

#### **E Special** - Boomer

> Creates an explosive effect that damages opponents around you and pushes them violently away.

#### **Q Special** - Black Hole Bomb

> Shoots a projectile that detonates at range, creating an intense gravity well that brings opponents toward the center, damaging them severely.

### Shields

#### Fortress Shield

> When activated, shield capacitor is fully charged. Each shot that hits the shield drains the capacitor based on how much damage is stopped. The shield only stops a threshold of damage...remaining damage will bleed through. Area effect damage is not stopped by this shield.

#### Reflection Shield

> When activated, shield capacitor is fully charged. Each shot that hits the shield is bounced back toward its attacker. All damage is stopped and the reflected shot may hit enemies. This takes a lot more energy than the Fortress Shield so it drains more quickly. Area effect damage is not stopped by this shield.

## Ranger

### Camps

> Camps are an important part to a rangers tool kit as it allows you to setup respawn points, allows for players to be healed by /dance, depending on your camp size it creates crafting stations, and at least currently as of (5/24/25) camps are required to create homesteads (though this will at some point not be the case).

> **TIP:** As of now (5/24/25) you cannot remove the yellow boundaries for a camp, only a homestead. So sorry photographers and videographers.

### Pathfinder Tool

> With the Pathfinder tool equipped it will allow you to locate survey nodes on the map. The current implementation as of (05/24/25) requires you to be within ~30m before the node reveals itself. The mini-game to get close enough is a type of hot/cold game, as you get closer to the node, an audio cue starts beeping, as you get closer to the node the beeping becomes quicker, as you get further away the beeping becomes slower. 

> Use the LMB to click a survey node to record it. Once recorded the node will turn from orange to green.

> When you identify all the survey nodes on a map you unlock a recipe to craft a trophy. Each planet has its own trophy:

#### **E Special** - Local Waypoint

> Creates a yellow marker that will denote the distance you are from the marker. It can be seen anywhere on the map.

### Skill: Survey Map

> Currently as of (05/24/25) it is an ascii map that will allow you to identify nodes that you have yet to discover.

### Trailblazer Tool

> Using the LMB you are able to send up a flare into the sky. Great for keeping track of your fellow reachers while exploring. Also great for parties during the night sky for firework like festivities.

#### Clearing Mode

##### **Q Special** - Flamethrower

> Use the flamethrower to burn trees, thickets, bushes, and flowers. You can also thaw frozen things or set creatures on fire.

#### **E Special** - Freeze Ray

> Use the freeze ray to put out fires or freeze liquids so you can walk across them.
