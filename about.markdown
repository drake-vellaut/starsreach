## Key Maps

### Movement

> - **W, A, S, D** - used for movement of your character
- **T** - Toggles you between free cursor or locked cursor mode
- **N** - Toggles you slow walking or normal walking speed
- **Ctrl** - Crouches if you do not have a tool equipped else while moving allows you to dodge
- ** Shift HOLD** - Allows you to sprint (sprinting drains your stamina - yellow bar)
- ** Space Bar** - Jump
- ** Space Bar HOLD** - If grav mesh is equipped, you will activate your grav mesh (this will drain your stamina- yellow bar)
- **`** - Auto run toggle.

### Tools

> - **LMB** - Based on the tool, the mode and the functionality you have selected, the tool will be used when you click LMB
- **1,2,3,4** - Allows you to select which tool you want to use. Tools can be equipped via your inventory only while in any homestead or camp.
- **Tab** - Switching between the different modes of your equipped tool.
- **Q and E** - Tool Dependent but allows for different tool functionality based on the mode you have selected.
- **F HOLD** - Opens a selection wheel and allows you to select which consumable you wish to map to your F key for quick usage
- **F** - Uses your consumable you selected via the selection wheel
- **R** - Allows you to use your grapple, it has a range of roughly ~40m
- **C** - If you have a shield equipped, this is the key to use that shield

### Functional Keys

> - **K** - Opens your skill tree, this is how you upgrade your skills
- **G** - Interact Key, this allows you to interact with crafting stations and other players when you are close enough
- **I** - Opens your inventory, which also has a tab for available receipes as well. This is where you also are able to equip your clothing items like grav mesh or shields (clothing tab on the right side)

### Useful Keys

> - **F4** - Hides your UI
- **F5** - Reloads your UI, useful if nameplates are not appearing or other UI issues. This will reset your entire UI so any chat conversations will be gone. This also has a chance of crashing you and varies from player to player on how often it occurs.
- **F10** - Upscale Screenshot that saves to your ~/Pictures/PlayableWorlds folder
- **Ctrl+o** - Hides your cursor, useful for grabbing screenshots
- **F9** - Displays your framerate information in the top left hand corner of your screen
