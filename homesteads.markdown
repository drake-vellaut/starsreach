## Homesteads

>

### How to unlock them

> Currently as of (5/24/25) you must use Ranger XP to unlock Homesteading in the Ranger Skill Tree. You can unlock up to three (3) homesteads per character, which there are a maximum of 4 characters you can have on a single account. So you could have a maximum of 12 homesteads if you max out all your homesteading skills on the maximum number of characters for an account.

### Placing Homesteads

> 1. You need a camp kit (as of (5/24/25) you start out with 3 camp kits). You can also craft camp kits with T1 metals and gemstones.
2. Open your inventory and RMB click your camp kit.
3. Select your location in the world and LMB click once you found your ideal location.
4. At the center of your camp you must go and click G to interact with your camp, here you will select "Upgrade to Homestead"
5. You now have your homestead

> **TIP:** For combining homesteads or larger builds take a look at [Techniques for Placing](#techniques-for-placing) this will help you understand how to place homesteads to make those 3 homestead houses, shops, etc

> **TIP:** Be sure you have enough homestead skills unlocked before placing your camp kit, else when you try to interact with your homestead, you may receive a message stating you are at your maximum number of homesteads

> **TIP:** Your camp kits when placed my look not even or off kilter, but once you turn it into a homestead it will be straight.

### Techniques for Placing

>

#### Depositing and Lining Up

#### Higher than 255 height limit

> You are able to still place homesteads and build with fabricator pieces above the 255m sim height limit. BUT you will not be able to use depositing or instaformer blocks.

> But with a grav mesh, at least three (3) stamina boosters, and a bit of patience you can place them so they are perfectly aligned.

1. Ensure you have a grav mesh,at least three (3) stamina booster equipped to your F Key, and your stamina is full (if you have wounds ensure someone /dance for you so you can be at maximum stamina)
2. Stand on the green holo-like device of the homestead at or near the maximum 255m height limit.
3. Switch to your pathfinder tool, you must have local waypoint skill unlocked (this step is not necessary but will greatly help give you an idea of how high you are for later steps). And set your local waypoint on the green holo-like device.
4. Open your inventory and select a camp kit with RMB
5. Toggle your mouse mode with T so that you are in free cursor mode.
6. Hold space bar and go straight up, keeping your cursor on the green holo-like device.
7. When you reach about 1/2 stamina click F to start using your stamina boosters, try to keep your stamina bar above 1/4-1/2.
8. Once you around roughly ~90m above your waypoint start LMB clicking rapidly as you continue to ascend, be sure to still being going straight up and your cursor is still on the green holo-like device
9. And once you get around ~110m your camp will place, but you must be clicking rapidly else you may be off a few meters on alignment.
10. Fall down back onto the newly placed center camp and G interact and "Upgrade to Homestead"
11. Verify perfect alignment, else if it is not aligned G interact and "Release Homestead" and repeat steps 1-11 again.
12. You have successfully placed the homestead above the 255 height limit! Congrats!

> **TIP:** The maximum distance a homestead can be places as of (5/24/25) is 100m, that is why we fly up 100m and are rapidly clicking to place so it place at exactly 100m above our homestead height.

> **TIP:** If you are curious on your homestead height location, jump on your green holo-like device and type "/loc" and look at your Y: coordinate and then add 11 to that number and that is your maximum height of your homestead. If that is above 255, then you will need to use this method or another method not involving depositing or instaformer blocks to place homesteads further up.
